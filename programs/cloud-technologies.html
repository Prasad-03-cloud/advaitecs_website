    Cloud Training Programs

    Take your cloud skills to the next level with our structured and industry-aligned training program, offering hands-on experience, classroom sessions, personalized coaching, and real-world exercises. Whether you're just starting or looking to build development expertise in cloud platforms, our dual-track approach—Foundational and Developer—ensures a seamless learning journey for all skill levels.

    Foundational Track:

    Cloud Technologies for Beginners

    This track is designed for individuals who are new to cloud computing or transitioning into the tech space. It provides a solid grounding in the core concepts and services of Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP).

    What You Will Learn:

    • Introduction to Cloud Computing & Deployment Models
    • Overview of AWS, Azure & GCP – similarities, differences & use cases
    • Understanding cloud service models – IaaS, PaaS, SaaS
    • Basics of compute, storage, networking & databases in the cloud
    • Introduction to cloud billing, pricing models & cost optimization
    • Identity & Access Management (IAM) fundamentals
    • Hands-on labs: Setting up your first cloud environment

    Delivery Mode:

    • Classroom-based sessions with expert instructors
    • Personalized coaching to address individual learning needs
    • Interactive exercises & real-world simulations for hands-on practice

    Ideal For:

    Students, entry-level IT professionals, or anyone looking to understand and explore the world of cloud computing.

    Developer Track:

    Cloud Development & Integration

    This advanced track is tailored for developers and IT professionals aiming to build, deploy, and manage cloud-native applications. It dives deep into service integration, automation, and real-world application development using AWS, Azure, and GCP tools.

    What You Will Learn:

    • Cloud-native application architecture & design patterns
    • Developing scalable applications using AWS Lambda, Azure Functions, and GCP Cloud Functions
    • Building CI/CD pipelines with services like AWS CodePipeline, Azure DevOps, and Google Cloud Build
    • Working with containers: Docker, Kubernetes, and cloud-managed services (EKS, AKS, GKE)
    • Serverless computing and microservices design
    • Integration with APIs, databases, and third-party services
    • Monitoring, logging, and performance tuning of cloud applications
    • Security best practices for cloud-based development

    Delivery Mode:

    • In-depth classroom training with structured modules
    • One-on-one mentorship for complex topics and career guidance
    • Capstone projects and practical labs to simulate enterprise use-cases

    Ideal For:

    Software developers, DevOps engineers, system architects, and IT professionals aiming to advance their cloud development skills and prepare for certifications or career growth.

    Why Choose Us?

    • Industry-experienced trainers with real-world project exposure
    • Practical approach with 70% hands-on learning
    • Certification guidance for AWS, Azure, and GCP tracks
    • Small batch sizes for personalized attention
    • Post-training support and mentorship
